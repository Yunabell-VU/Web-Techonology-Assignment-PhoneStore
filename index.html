Compatibility

This stylesheet is designed based on Chrome.
For other browsers, there are several differences in terms of visual effects.
For example, in Safari, the font-monospace for #logo can't display as expected, a better way to deal with this is to change logo into a picture or choose another font.
Another difference we observed between browsers is the border of rows in table. Chrome can display border effect correctly in terms of row by row, however, in IE, it displays borders of every td which should be tr and in Safari, it simply hidden the border. We haven't found out the solution yet.
